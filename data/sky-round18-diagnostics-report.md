### High-Value Opportunities

1. **Lab ordering API platforms** — Redox, Health Gorilla, Availity are building the "Stripe for labs"
2. **Cloud-native PACS** — Ambra, Pro Medicus displacing legacy on-prem
3. **AI triage** — Aidoc, Viz.ai winning in stroke/trauma workflow
4. **Digital pathology** — Proscia, PathAI disrupting slide-based workflow
5. **POC connectivity** — Untapped integration layer for all these devices

- **Unified lab+imaging ordering** — No dominant cross-domain platform
- **Global lab interoperability** — Regional fragmentation persists
- **Consumer lab data routing** — DTC results to EHR is broken
- **POC device cloud** — Siloed by manufacturer
