## Key Patterns Identified

### 1. Digital Specialty Pathways
- Digital MSK (Hinge, Kaia, SWORD) creating end-to-end orthopedic care pathways
- Teledermatology (First Derm, Spruce) enabling remote specialty access
- Telecardiology (Tricog) democratizing cardiac expertise globally

### 2. AI-Powered Diagnostics Democratizing Access
- AI ultrasound (Caption Health) enabling primary care cardiac imaging
- AI ECG (iRhythm, AliveCor, Eko) creating referral pathways
- AI dermoscopy (DermEngine) supporting skin cancer screening

### 3. Episode-Based Care Coordination
- Orthopedic episode management (Force Therapeutics)
- Cancer care navigation (Navigating Cancer)
- Pre-hab and optimization (PeerWell)

### 4. Automated Detection + Routing
- Stroke/cardiac emergency coordination (Viz.ai)
- Continuous monitoring with AI alerts (BioTelemetry, Preventice)
- This is the **closest model to Varden's vision**

### 5. Specialty Network Models
- US Oncology Network aggregating independent practices
- Shared services and economies of scale
- Model for specialty practice coordination
