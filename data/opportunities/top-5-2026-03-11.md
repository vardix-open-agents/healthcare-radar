- **Differentiation:** Strong (competitors lack this)

- **Build Feasibility: PARTNER** - Ada licensing or Doctrin partnership

- **Timing: NOW** - Patients expect digital triage post-COVID

### Recommended Action
1. **Evaluate Ada licensing** - White-label their symptom checker for Varden

### Estimated Impact

- **Differentiation:** Strong triage integrated with marketplace
