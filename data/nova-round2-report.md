### 3. Three Strategic Insights

**Insight 1: The Great Bifurcation**
Healthcare companies are splitting into "infrastructure plays" (Leyr, Zymego) vs "clinical plays" (Kry, Doctolib). Varden should position as infrastructure-first to avoid competing with €400M+ funded clinical players.

**Insight 2: The SME Gold Rush**
80% of UK businesses are SMEs, but occupational health penetration is near zero. HealthPod (UK) and Sential (Netherlands) are the ONLY digital players targeting this segment. Blue ocean.

**Insight 3: The Platform Wars**
Kry/Livi, Doctolib, and DocPlanner are consolidating Europe. Varden can't out-platform them. Instead: pick verticals they ignore (SME OH) and geographies they under-penetrate (Nordic small clinics).
