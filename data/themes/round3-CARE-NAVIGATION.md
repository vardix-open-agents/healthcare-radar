## 🎯 Problem Statement

Patients struggle to navigate the complex healthcare system: finding the right provider, booking appointments, and understanding where to seek appropriate care. This leads to:

- Delayed treatment
- Overuse of emergency services
- Underutilized specialist capacity
- Patient frustration and churn

## 🔑 Key Insights

### 1. This is a CROWDED but WINNER-TAKE-ALL market
- Doctolib dominates France/Germany
- Sweden is still fragmented (opportunity!)
- UK has AccuRx at 98% GP penetration

### 2. Integration is the moat
- Winners have deep EHR/practice management integrations
- Standalone booking = commodity
- Value is in the **workflow**, not just scheduling

### 3. Triage + Booking = Power combo
- Companies doing both (Doctrin, Ada) score highest
- Pure booking plays are vulnerable
- Patient journey integration is key
