## 🎯 Problem Statement

Patients cannot easily find, compare, and choose healthcare providers. Unlike hotels or restaurants, healthcare has no good "Yelp" or "TripAdvisor". Patients face:

- Information asymmetry (quality unknown)
- Difficulty comparing providers
- No transparent pricing
- Fragmented reviews and ratings
