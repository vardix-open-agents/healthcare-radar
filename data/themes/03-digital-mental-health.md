# Theme: Digital Mental Health Platforms

## Summary
Platforms offering digital mental health services including therapy, coaching, self-help programs, and specialized treatment for conditions like depression, addiction, and anxiety.

## Companies
- **Braive** (Sweden) - AI-driven mental health with evidence-based therapy programs. Part of AI Sweden startup community.
- **Monsenso** (Denmark) - AI-personalized depression treatment using behavioral data. Funded by Innovation Fund Denmark.
- **Kontigo Care** (Sweden) - AI-powered addiction treatment (Previct®). Public company, returning to growth with profitability.
- **HjälpProgrammet** (Sweden) - CBT-based self-help program. Healthcare region partnerships.
- **Talkspace** (US/EU) - Public company. Text/video therapy. European expansion.
- **Mindable** (Germany) - DTx for panic disorder. DiGA pathway.
- **Brain+** (Denmark) - Cognitive therapy for dementia. Public (Spotlight Stock Market). Ayla CST Assistant.
- **BetterUp** (US/EU) - Digital coaching for enterprises. $300M+ funding.

## Growth Signal
**HIGH** - Mental health is a crisis globally. COVID accelerated demand. Nordic countries have high awareness but long wait times for public services.

## Evidence
- 8 companies in knowledge base (largest single category)
- Talkspace: Public company
- BetterUp: $300M+ funding
- Kontigo Care: Public, profitable growth
- Brain+: Public company
- Multiple Swedish players = active local market

## Drivers
- Mental health crisis post-COVID
- Long wait times in public mental health services
- Destigmatization of therapy
- Employer wellness programs (B2B)
- Regulatory pathways (DiGA in Germany) creating reimbursement
- Digital-native population comfortable with app-based therapy

## Varden Fit
**MODERATE** - Mental health is adjacent to primary care but not core infrastructure. Opportunities:
- Integrate mental health providers into Varden marketplace
- Offer mental health triage alongside physical health
- Partner with specialized platforms for referrals

However, Varden's strength is infrastructure/marketplace, not clinical content. Better to partner than build.

## Opportunity
1. **Marketplace integration** - Add mental health providers to Varden's network. Fastest path to offering mental health without building clinical content.
2. **Referral partnerships** - Partner with Braive, Monsenso, or HjälpProgrammet for patient referrals from primary care.
3. **B2B employer packages** - Bundle mental health with occupational health (Digna model) for employer clients.
4. **Specialized screening** - Integrate mental health screening into intake/triage workflow.

**Partnership opportunity rather than build. Mental health is a different competency than marketplace infrastructure.**
