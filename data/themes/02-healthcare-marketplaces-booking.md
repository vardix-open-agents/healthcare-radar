# Theme: Healthcare Marketplaces & Booking Platforms

## Summary
Platforms that connect patients with healthcare providers through search, comparison, and appointment booking - the core model that Varden operates in.

## Companies
- **Doctolib** (France) - European benchmark. 300K+ providers, 80M monthly visits. €500M+ funding. Operating in France, Germany, Italy.
- **Kryds** (Sweden) - Private healthcare marketplace for Swedish market. Direct local competitor.
- **Medevis** (Sweden) - Provider comparison with quality metrics and patient reviews. Differentiation through transparency.
- **Klaara** (Finland) - Finnish healthcare marketplace. Potential Nordic expansion target.
- **Zymego** (Sweden) - Automated waiting list filling for cancelled appointments. 5-20% no-show recovery.
- **Valley** (Sweden) - Practice management for specialist clinics with booking, video, and EHR integrations.

## Growth Signal
**HIGH** - Doctolib validates the model at massive scale. Swedish market is less consolidated. Kry/Livi and Min Doktor dominate telemedicine but not full marketplace.

## Evidence
- Doctolib: €500M+ funding, 300K providers, 80M monthly visits
- Zymego: Quantified problem (5-20% no-shows = millions of wasted slots)
- Multiple Swedish players = fragmented market = opportunity

## Drivers
- Patient demand for convenience and choice
- Private healthcare growth in Nordics
- Digital-first expectations post-COVID
- Provider need for efficient booking and reduced no-shows
- Quality transparency becoming expected

## Varden Fit
**CORE BUSINESS** - This IS Varden's model. Learnings:
- **Doctolib**: European benchmark for scale. Study their provider tools, patient features, and B2B offerings.
- **Zymego**: No-show recovery is a specific, high-value feature. Could integrate with Varden's booking system.
- **Medevis**: Quality metrics and reviews differentiate. Consider transparency features.
- **Klaara**: Finnish market opportunity for Nordic expansion.
- **Kryds**: Direct Swedish competitor - study their approach carefully.

## Opportunity
1. **Acquire or partner with Zymego** - Automated waitlist filling is a specific feature with clear ROI. Integrates directly into booking workflow.
2. **Nordic expansion via Klaara** - Finnish market entry through partnership or acquisition.
3. **Learn from Doctolib** - Deep study of their provider tools, monetization, and patient features. What can Varden adapt?
4. **Quality differentiation** - If Medevis gains traction, consider review/quality features as competitive response.
5. **Specialist focus** - Valley shows specialist clinic value. Varden could strengthen specialist provider tools.

**Marketplace is Varden's core - these are competitors and benchmarks to study closely.**
