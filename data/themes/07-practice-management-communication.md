# Theme: Practice Management & Patient Communication

## Summary
Tools that help healthcare providers manage their operations - scheduling, patient communication, workflow automation, and care coordination.

## Companies
- **AccuRx** (UK) - GP communication platform. SMS, online consultations, NHS integrations. Used by 98% of GP practices in England. $35M+ funding.
- **Doctrin** (Sweden) - Digital patient communication with automated medical history, AI triage, case management. Multi-country (Sweden, Norway, Czech Republic, UK, France).
- **Valley** (Sweden) - Practice management for specialist clinics. Scheduling, patient portal, video, EHR integrations.
- **Allvi** (Sweden) - AI-powered resource planning and allocation. Incubated at Uppsala Innovation Centre. Focus on both patient care and staff conditions.
- **Curoflow** (Sweden) - Care pathway orchestration connecting providers. Patient app, provider dashboard. Pharmaceutical patient support programs.

## Growth Signal
**HIGH** - AccuRx's 98% UK penetration proves demand. Swedish market is fragmented. Practice tools = sticky infrastructure.

## Evidence
- AccuRx: 98% of English GP practices = massive scale
- Doctrin: Multi-country deployment, 40+ employees
- Multiple Swedish players = fragmented but active market
- Infrastructure plays = high retention, lower churn

## Drivers
- Phone line overwhelm at GP practices
- Patient expectations for digital communication
- Administrative burden on healthcare staff
- Need for workflow automation
- Care coordination across providers
- Staff burnout and retention

## Varden Fit
**VERY HIGH** - Practice management is core to Varden's clinic value proposition:
- Clinics need efficient operations
- Varden's booking + EHR integrations are practice management adjacent
- Strong practice tools = clinic retention on Varden's platform

This is infrastructure that makes Varden essential to clinic operations.

## Opportunity
1. **Study AccuRx deeply** - 98% UK penetration is extraordinary. What features drove adoption? How do they handle NHS integrations? Applicable lessons for Swedish EHR integrations.

2. **Integrate Doctrin or acquire** - Doctrin has triage, communication, and case management. Could integrate with Varden's booking to create end-to-end patient journey. Potential acquisition target.

3. **Build Allvi-style resource planning** - AI-powered scheduling optimization. Uses Varden's booking data to help clinics optimize capacity.

4. **Care pathway orchestration** - Curoflow's model of coordinating care across providers is exactly what Varden's marketplace needs. Partner or build.

5. **Valley for specialists** - Valley focuses on specialist clinics. Varden could acquire or partner to strengthen specialist offerings.

**Practice management is high-fit infrastructure. Prioritize partnerships or features in this area.**
