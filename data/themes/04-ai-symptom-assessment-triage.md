# Theme: AI Symptom Assessment & Triage

## Summary
AI-powered tools that help patients understand their symptoms and route them to appropriate care - either self-care, primary care, specialist, or emergency.

## Companies
- **Ada Health** (Germany) - AI symptom assessment chatbot. 15M+ users. $90M+ funding. CE marked Class IIa. 7 languages.
- **Babylon Health** (UK) - AI triage + virtual GP. GP at Hand NHS registration. Public company (SPAC). Caution: financial challenges.
- **Corti** (Denmark) - Real-time AI for emergency call triage. $60M Series B. Deployed in Denmark, Sweden, UK.
- **MindDoktor** (Sweden) - Swedish-language symptom checker adapted to local healthcare pathways.
- **Navamed** (Norway) - Health navigation platform helping patients find appropriate care.
- **Doctrin** (Sweden) - Digital triage + case management for healthcare providers. Multi-country deployment.

## Growth Signal
**HIGH** - Symptom assessment is table stakes for modern digital health. Ada validates global scale. Swedish localization is key differentiator.

## Evidence
- Ada Health: 15M users, $90M+ funding, global reach
- Corti: $60M Series B, emergency dispatch deployments
- Babylon: Public company (though struggling financially)
- Multiple Swedish-focused players showing local market opportunity

## Drivers
- Patient confusion about where to seek care
- Overcrowded emergency departments
- Primary care access bottlenecks
- Self-triage demand (patients want guidance before booking)
- AI/LLM maturity making conversational triage viable

## Varden Fit
**HIGH** - Triage is directly in the booking journey:
1. Patient arrives at Varden
2. Symptom checker guides them
3. Appropriate provider is recommended
4. Booking is completed

This is the perfect top-of-funnel feature for a healthcare marketplace. It improves patient outcomes AND drives bookings to the right providers.

## Opportunity
1. **Partner with Ada Health** - License or white-label their technology. They have the most mature, regulatory-cleared solution. Could integrate directly into Varden's booking flow.

2. **Build Swedish-localized triage** - MindDoktor and Doctrin show the value of local adaptation. Varden could build lightweight triage optimized for Swedish healthcare pathways and Varden's provider network.

3. **Learn from Corti** - Their real-time audio triage is advanced but focused on emergency. Could inspire lighter-weight video/chat triage for primary care intake.

4. **Integrate with booking** - The key is connecting triage output directly to booking. "Based on your symptoms, we recommend these 3 providers with availability tomorrow."

**Triage + booking = powerful combination. This should be a priority feature for Varden.**
